Story

June 15, 2005

Courtney & Nick attended Titusville High School together and began dating shortly after graduation in 2005, having met through mutual friends (William & John, members of the wedding party). Their first year was spent at medium-distance, with Nick attending UCF and Courtney finishing her Associate's at BCC — she joined him in Orlando the following year.

The couple moved into an apartment together in 2008 as Nick finished his degree in Digital Media and began his career, while Courtney progressed to her Psychology Master's program shortly thereafter.

In early 2010, the pair added a new family member — an 8-week old puppy named Wesley (a Westie). On July 16, 2010, after 5 years together, Nick popped the question. Wedding plans commenced as Courtney completed her Master's and subsequently began her own career.
